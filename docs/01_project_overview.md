# Project Overview

## Project Title
Weather Impact on Beverage Sales – Budapest Cafés (2023)

## Objective
The goal of this project is to analyze how **weather conditions (temperature and precipitation)** influence **consumer purchasing behavior** for hot and cold beverages in Budapest cafés.

By enriching synthetic café sales data with real historical weather data, the project demonstrates how external environmental factors affect demand patterns and revenue generation.

## Key Questions
- How do hot and cold beverage sales vary with temperature?
- Are cold days associated with higher hot beverage consumption?
- Do hot days significantly increase cold beverage sales?
- Can these patterns be observed consistently across months?

## Scope
- Location: Budapest, Hungary
- Time period: January 1, 2023 – December 31, 2023
- Data granularity: Daily sales and daily weather data
