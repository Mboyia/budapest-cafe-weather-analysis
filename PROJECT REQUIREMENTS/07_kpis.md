# Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The project contributes to measuring the following KPIs:

## 1. Beverage Sales Volume by Temperature
- Measures total units sold under cold, mild, and hot conditions.
- Indicates how weather influences customer demand.

## 2. Revenue by Beverage Category and Weather
- Tracks revenue contribution of hot and cold beverages under different temperatures.
- Supports pricing and promotion strategies.

## 3. Seasonal Demand Shift Index
- Compares sales patterns across months and temperature categories.
- Identifies seasonal transitions and peak demand periods.

These KPIs help stakeholders align inventory, marketing, and pricing decisions
with external environmental factors.
